Why we are innovating the way music is being discovered...
Playlister.Club was founded by a group of experienced music industry veterans and technology experts who wanted to prove a shared belief:
Playlisters are the music industry's new A&Rs.
The Playlister.Club founders contemplated that the true power of playlists had yet to be harnessed.
Leveraging tech to create community
In 2019, they unveiled the world’s most advanced playlister toolset; revolutionizing how playlisters find new music and create better playlists. This allowed them to capture a vibrant community of playlisters, who are passionate about curating & discovering new music.

Mission: to help music creators grow.
Combining the playlister’s collective tastes, music knowledge, and data, Playlister.Club reset the music industry’s expectations of what independent playlists mean, and how they can be leveraged. With their platform that has helped +400,000 songs get placed so far, the Playlister.Club team is tirelessly innovating new ways to achieve the same mission: to help music creators grow.
